A free and open source App for planning non-technical dives using either French Navy MN90 tables or Bühlmann ZHL-16C with Gradient Factors.

### Usage

*   **Drag up/down:** Adjust value.
*   **Tap:** Open value list.
*   **Double tap:** Reset value.
*   **Modes:** MN90 (Tables) or GF (Bühlmann ZHL-16C).
*   **Calculations:** TTS (Time To Surface) and Reserve (end of dive tank pressure).
*   **Alerts:** Reserve < 50b (red), ppO2 > 1.5 (orange).

### Calculation assumptions

*   Sea level, with seawater
*   **Profile:** square dive
*   **Descent rate:** 20 m/min
*   **Ascent rate:** 15 m/min
*   **Rate between stops:** 6 m/min
*   **No safety stop**

### Installation

Can be installed on mobile/laptop/desktop and used offline. Direct installation from the browsers (no PlayStore, no AppStore).

*   **Android/Desktop:** Use the install prompt or '+' icon in the address bar.
*   **iOS:** In Safari, tap Share -> 'Add to Home Screen'.
