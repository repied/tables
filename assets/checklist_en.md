## Gear (Boat)
* Mask, fins, snorkel
* Wetsuit, boots, gloves
* Weights (belt/pockets)
* BCD (Buoyancy Control Device)
* Regulators (primary + octopus)
* Dive computer & compass
* SMB (Surface Marker Buoy) & reel
* Light, knife, whistle
* Logbook, certification card, medical

## Buddy Check (BWRAF)
* **B**CD : inflator and dumps tested
* **W**eights : right hand release, secure
* **R**eleases : all buckles secure
* **A**ir : breathing on both regs, gauge check
* **F**inal check : gear ready, mask/fins on

## Dive Planning
* Max depth and bottom time
* Turn pressure & reserve
* Planned stops & TTS
* Separated from buddy procedure
* Communication signals

## Boat Safety
* Oxygen kit & First aid kit
* VHF (Channel 16) & Phone
* Alpha flag
* Passenger list (Manifest)
* Drinking water & Emergency blanket

## Emergency Procedures
* **Out of Air:** Signal, take buddy's octopus, ascend together
* **Lost Buddy:** Look for 1 min, safe ascent, reunite on surface
* **Breathlessness:** Signal, stop, calm down, breathe
* **Decompression Incident:** Alert (VHF/112), Oxygen, hydration, evacuation
