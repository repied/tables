A free and open source application for planning dives, using either MN90 tables or Gradient Factors.

### Usage

*   **Drag up/down** or **Tap:** Adjust value
*   **Double tap:** Reset value
*   **Modes:** MN90 (Tables) or GF (Bühlmann ZHL-16C)
*   **Calculations:** Total Ascent Time and end-of-dive pressure
*   **Alerts:** Reserve < 50b (red), ppO2 > 1.5 (orange)

### Calculation assumptions

*   Recreational square dive at sea level, in salt water
*   **Descent:** 20 m/min
*   **Ascent:** 10 m/min
*   **Between stops:** 6 m/min
*   **No safety stop**

### Installation

Direct installation from the browser (no PlayStore, no AppStore) for  offline usage.

*   **Android/Computer:** Use the install prompt or the '+' icon in the address bar.
*   **iOS:** In Safari, tap Share -> 'Add to Home Screen'.
